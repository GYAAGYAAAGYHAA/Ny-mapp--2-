Richard Sundevik signed with Red Bull Junior

Richard Sundevik. Photos by Miran Evci.

For Richard Sundevik, the 22-year-old Swedish-korean F2 wunderkind, working with Red Bull Junior Team has been a dream nearly a decade in the making. We at GQ took our precious time to talk to him. So the world gets to truly witness the why this will be amazing for him —Miran Evci

———

Sundevik: Nice to meet you—I'm a bit nervious as this is my first interview,

Interviewer: It's weird we have had our eyes on you for a long time, but for you to actually have come this far is amazing.

Sundevik: I guess, but like I'm really good so you shouldn't be suprised—I'm coming after you Verstappen and your reign.

Interviewer: Hahahah- you sure do have a clear vision infront of you.

Sundevik: Yeah, I know that after I've become the king of F2, F1 will be no problem.

Interviewer: Alright, lets start with the questions. What are your thoughts on being in F2 and not F1.

Sundevik: It's very clear that most people think of F1. It's what made the sport, But I actually think that starting in F2 is a lot harder and more respectable. You don't have the same fans as someone in F1 would have. So thats why I've come up with this thought that if I could conquer F2 I will conquer F1 with absolutely no problem.

Interviewer: That's a very intresting point, you're definitely right about F1 being more popular than F2. But you still have to have had some kind of idol to look up to that played in F1.

Sundevik: Ofcourse, I never meant that I look down at F1 drivers, it's just that I know that I'm better than them- one person I still look up to is Nikita Mazepin.

Interviewer: Oh really, why so?

Sundevik: I believe that Mazepin doesn't only believe in competing in sport but also just being a philosopher, I would say that he defently has good thoughts. People call him a wife beater without understanding the reasons behind it. I've recently read a book about stoicism, and one of the essentials is that "Virtue is sufficient for happiness", meaning that you just have to excerise your virtues, and you will be able to live a good life, no matter what conditions- as long as you put in everything you got, Mazepin cleary loved his wife, he took his time to show his emotions to her.

Interviewer: I guess so, but lets get back to F2.

Sundevik: oh, sorry- I get a bit crazy when I let my mind go free.

Interviewer: No worries, So lets get back to Red Bull Junior Team.

Sundevik: Alright.

Interviewer: So we know that you have clearly been a fan of the Red Bull Junior Team for a long time, how does it feel to finally be apart of it

Sundevik: I mean it's amazing ever since i was around like 10-12 years old I've adored them, I remember that I told my classmate Benjamin that I would be on it. He said something along the lines of "that's crazy, I don't wanna crush your dreams but you probably will not reach them".

Interviewer: Is that so?

Sundevik: Yeah but here I am, stronger than ever.

Interviewer: You sure are, it was fun interviewing you Richard, We will continue to watch your dominance in the F2 Sphere.

Sundevik: Thank you for having me.
